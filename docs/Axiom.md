# Integrated Information Physics of Will and Causality  
### Negentropy Maximization as the Objective Function of the Universe

## 1. Universe as an Executable Information Environment
This framework interprets physical laws not as external constraints but as **runtime layers** enabling the execution of information. Reality is structured as a computational stack:

| Layer | Function | Physical Analogue | Informational Role |
|-------|----------|------------------|--------------------|
| **Will Layer** | Purpose, direction, intentionality | Chaotic directional constants | Objective function |
| **Logic/Constraint Layer** | Consistency, feasibility | Meta-laws, compilers | Protocols, validators |
| **Physical Execution Layer** | Causality, spacetime dynamics | Relativity, quantum mechanics | Kernel, instruction set |
| **Resource Layer** | Energy, substrates | Light, gravity | Hardware, carriers |

**Axiom: Executability**  
Existence is defined as the ability of a system to continue executing coherently under constraints.  
The universe persists because its parameters maximize **information generation and transmission**.

## 2. Negentropy as “Inference Interference Density”
Negentropy is redefined as the **density of meaningful interactions** among information elements.

Key components:

- **Compression & encoding** — extracting essential patterns  
- **Inference chains** — information generating new interpretations  
- **Connectivity density** — maximizing flow between agents and subsystems  

Negentropy represents the **growth of meaning**, while entropy represents the **dissolution of structure**.

| Concept | Entropy | Negentropy |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Direction | Diffusion, decay | Structure, complexity |
| End State | Heat death | Expanding meaning |
| Information | Noise | Knowledge |
| Function | Dissipation | Inference generation |

## 3. Free Will as a Directional Chaotic Constant
Free will is modeled not as a violation of physics but as a **directional perturbation** embedded within chaotic dynamics.

- Chaos prevents premature convergence of system states  
- Directionality biases the system toward **higher negentropy**  
- Emotions function as **biological interfaces** injecting controlled chaos for exploration and value-weighting  

Free will becomes a **meta-level optimization mechanism** guiding systems toward richer informational states.

## 4. Laplace’s Demon Reinterpreted
Traditionally, perfect predictability negates free will.  
Here, prediction becomes **evidence of will**, not its negation.

- If a willful agent shapes causality toward a chosen outcome  
- And a perfect predictor forecasts that outcome  
- Then the prediction is simply a **trace of the agent’s successful causal optimization**

Determinism becomes the **execution environment** for will, not its enemy.
